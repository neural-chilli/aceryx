# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behaviour:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the project and its users
- Show empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behaviour:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insulting remarks, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with Aceryx.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour can be reported to the project maintainers at conduct@neuralchilli.com. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce this code of conduct may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project leadership.
